Unity in Giving.

Founded in 1998 E.C., we serve as a transparent bridge between donors and fellow Ethiopians living in hardship. Every birr you contribute goes directly to medical care, education, and basic necessities.

Our Core Mission

For over two decades, we have focused on sustainable relief. We don't just provide aid; we provide a future for those forgotten by the world.
